Valerie Thomas

1943 - present

Valerie Thomas is a former NASA project manager, scientest, inventor, and notable educational speaker. She made several contributions and led multiple projects during her decades working at NASA. In particular her creation of the Landsat program, as well as multiple media formats used in the program, are noteworthy. In 1980, she was granted the patent for her creation of the Illusion Transmitter -- a device used to project a 3d 'hologram' from a single image -- which is has been further adapted and is still in use by NASA today. Despite her various contributions to science, she has dedicated much of her time to speaking to students and future scientists at schools and conferences over the years.
