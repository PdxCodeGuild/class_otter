Bakar

12 February 1994 - Present

Bakar (Abubakar Baker Shariff-Farr), is a British singer, songwriter known for his experimental and genre spanning indie rock influenced style.
He was born in Camden North London, and has said that "going outside of London and hearing other tastes... definitely did something to him."

Quotes

  1. "It might start from a conversation or it might just happen randomly..."
  2. “I think kids coming up now will just make what they want to make.”
  3. "I’ve never been to Glastonbury with a ticket!""
