Welcome to OUR bookstore!

We are a small, privately owned bookstore bought and cared for with the intention of making a safe, comfy space for readers to come and enjoy a quiet place to get away and relax. Your business helps us do this, so in effect, you are only helping yourself by shopping here! We offer various amenities such as a comfy chair or lounger, a drink of your choice, even wine for those interested and of age. We are currently working on plans to add a fireplace, because who doesn't secretly have a fantasy of reading in a comfy chair next to a real fire? Do you have an idea of how to make this place better? Please, stop by a leave us a note or talk to me personally and we can see what'll happen!
