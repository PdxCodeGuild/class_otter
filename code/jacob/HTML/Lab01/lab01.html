John Ronald Reuel Tolkien

January 3rd, 1892 - September 2, 1973

J.R.R. Tolkien was and English writer, poet, philologist and academic best known as the author of The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings.

Places Tolkien lived:

  • Bloemfontein, South Africa
  • Kings Heath, England
  • Worcestershire, England
